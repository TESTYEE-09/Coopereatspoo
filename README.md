# Cooper Eats Poo: The Forbidden Five

A ridiculous full-screen Three.js story shooter hosted at **coopereatspoo.online**.

## Story

Snacktown has outlawed burgers, pizza and candy. Cooper fights through the Fruit Patrol, destroys the Wellness Laboratory, defeats Doctor Apple, and enters the Sewer Throne for a multi-phase final fight against King Poo.

## Features

- Three complete story chapters with dialogue and cinematic chapter cards
- Third-person movement, mouse aiming, shooting, dashing and mobile controls
- Checkpoint saving with Continue support
- Fruit and poo enemy waves with melee and ranged attacks
- Destructible Vitamin Reactors
- Doctor Apple and King Poo boss fights with three phases each
- Burger healing, pizza spread-shot and candy rapid-fire powerups
- Procedural Three.js environments with bloom, shadows, particles and screen shake
- No downloaded art assets or model files

## Controls

- `WASD` or arrow keys: move
- Mouse: aim
- Left click or `Space`: fire
- `Shift`: dash
- `E`: use a stored burger medkit
- `Escape`: pause

Touch controls appear automatically on mobile devices.

## Hosting

The site deploys from `main` through the GitHub Pages workflow in `.github/workflows/pages.yml`.

Custom domain: `coopereatspoo.online`
